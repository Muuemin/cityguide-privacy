Privacy Policy

City Guide

Last Updated: 2024

1. Introduction

At City Guide ("we", "our", "app"), we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, use, and share when you use the City Guide mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1. Personal Information

When you use our app, we may collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Email address, name, profile photo
  • Authentication: Google or Apple account information (depending on your login method)
  • User Content: Reviews, ratings, favorites, bookings

2.2. Location Information

The app collects country and city information you select. This information is used to show you nearby places and personalize our services. Sensitive location data (GPS coordinates) is only collected with your permission and when using map features.

2.3. Usage Data

We may collect the following usage data:

  • In-app interactions and navigation
  • Places added to favorites
  • Bookings made
  • Reviews and ratings written

2.4. Device Information

We may collect technical information such as device type, operating system version, and app version to ensure the app functions properly.

3. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain app services
  • To create and manage your account
  • To process your bookings
  • To provide personalized content and recommendations
  • To improve user experience
  • For security and fraud prevention
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for commercial purposes. Your information may only be shared in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Our service providers such as Firebase (Google) provide the infrastructure necessary for the app to function. These service providers are subject to their own privacy policies.
  • Legal Requirements: In case of a legal obligation or court order
  • Security: For security, fraud prevention, or to protect our legal rights

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. We protect your data using secure infrastructure providers like Firebase. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal data, subject to our legal obligations and legitimate business interests.

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our mobile app does not use cookies. However, third-party services like Firebase may use their own analytics and tracking technologies.

8. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access Right: Access your collected personal data
  • Correction Right: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion Right: Delete your account and data
  • Objection Right: Object to data processing
  • Data Portability: Transfer your data to another service

You can contact us to exercise these rights.

9. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that we have collected personal information from a child, we will immediately delete that information.

10. Privacy Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email. The effective date of changes will be indicated at the top of this page.

11. Contact

For privacy-related questions:

Email: privacy@cityguide.app

Support: support@cityguide.app
