Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I register for the app?
When you open the app, click the "Sign Up" button on the login screen. You can register with your email address and password, or quickly sign in with your Google/Apple account.
I forgot my password, what should I do?
Click the "Forgot Password" link on the login screen. A password reset link will be sent to your email address. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder.
How do I make a booking?
Go to a place's detail page, click the "Make Booking" button. Select date, time, and number of guests, then confirm your booking. Your booking can be viewed from your profile page.
How can I add to favorites?
You can add places to your favorites by clicking the heart icon on a place's detail page. You can access your favorites from your profile page.
I'm a business owner, how can I add my venue?
Select "Business Registration" from your profile page and fill in the required information. After your application is approved, you can add and manage your venue.
The app is crashing or running slowly, what should I do?
First, try closing and reopening the app. If the problem persists, update the app or restart your device. If you're still experiencing issues, contact us.
How can I delete my account?
Go to "Account Settings" from your profile page and select "Delete Account". Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.
